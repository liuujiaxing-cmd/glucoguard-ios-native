# App Icon 生成提示词 (Midjourney / DALL-E)

你可以将以下提示词复制到 Midjourney、DALL-E 3 或 Stable Diffusion 中，生成高质量的 App 图标。

## 方案 A：极简医疗风 (推荐)
> **Prompt:**
> An app icon for a diabetes management iOS app called "GlucoGuard". Minimalist design, a stylized shield combined with a blood drop or a heart shape. Primary color is medical blue (#0052CC) with soft gradients. White background. Apple App Store style, glossy finish, rounded corners, high quality, vector style, 8k resolution. --v 6.0

## 方案 B：温暖关怀风 (适合公益)
> **Prompt:**
> An app icon for a health app. A warm and caring design featuring a heart shape protected by a gentle shield. Soft blue and teal color palette. Friendly, approachable, clean lines. 3D render style, soft lighting, matte finish. White background. --v 6.0

## 方案 C：科技智能风 (强调 AI)
> **Prompt:**
> iOS app icon for an AI-powered health tracker. Abstract representation of a glucose molecule connecting with a digital brain or spark. Modern, futuristic, gradient blue and purple colors. Tech style, clean, simple, vector art. --v 6.0

---

## 导出建议
1.  **尺寸**：生成后请裁剪为 **1024x1024 px** 的正方形 PNG。
2.  **圆角**：上传 App Store 时请使用**直角 (无圆角)** 的图片，Apple 会自动切圆角。
3.  **透明度**：图标**不能**包含透明区域 (Alpha 通道)，必须是实底。
